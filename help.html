FAQ

Consents are agreements managed by parents for their child’s data to be used or seen by specific Service Providers.

Depending on the type of consents, the consents are mainly used for generating learning analytics for future research purposes for improving the education system in Finland. New Programs or changes in the education can be done based on the data gathered from your child.

Again depending on the type of consents required, it may be erased or stored in the database for further use in the future.

In the Active Consent page, you will be able to see the list of approved and active consents, you can always change your mind by clicking ‘edit’ to decline the approval of consent made previously. Then, data will not be collected from your child.

Data collected previously will not be taken away from the database after being collected. However, it will not be used or collected if consent is set to ‘not active’.
